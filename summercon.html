SummerCon CRCE 2023

SUMMERCON CRCE 2023 is Fr. Conceicao Rodrigues College of Engineering's Flagship Event in Mumbai, India by HAWK-i CRCE which provides security professionals & Enthusiasts with a place to learn the very latest in information security risks, research, and trends. Leading security researchers from around the globe present their most recent findings and vulnerabilities in a friendly, vendor-neutral environment. Attendees will learn about cutting edge research on a variety of subjects, from flaws in common everything in between.

  1. 1. Talks and workshops from various speakers from various companies, security communities, and industries.
  2. 2. Networking Opportunities
  3. 3. Panel Discussion with Industry Experts.
  4. 4. Job Fair
